Muzzammil (MZ)

Zaveri

Partner

Muzzammil is a Partner at Gradient Ventures.

Muzzammil began his career co-founding Proxino, a Y Combinator (S11) backed startup in the JavaScript error detection space.

Prior to joining Gradient, Muzzammil was part of the venture team at Kleiner Perkins Caufield and Byers (KPCB). While there, he was actively involved with investments in BuddyBuild (acquired by Apple), Incorta, FullStory, UJET, Figma, Zumper and Feather. During his tenure there he helped spearhead their renewed seed stage investing efforts.

Muzzammil was featured on 2018 Forbes 30 Under 30 in Venture Capital. He holds a BA in Computer Science from the University of Virginia.
